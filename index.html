Types of Diabetes

Diabetes comes in four main types These types consist of : Type 2 Diabetes - This type is the most common, and affects the way your body handles glucose. This type is classified into two types. Prediabetes - This type of diabetes isn't exactly type two, but the blood sugar in them is very high, but not high enough to be considered Type 2. Gestational diabetes - This diabetes is type two, but only affects women when they are pregnant.
